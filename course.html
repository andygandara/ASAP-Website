link   Possible Project Topics with Dr. A.A. Rodriguez
  • embedded sensing and decision making, modelling and control of complex systems
  • algorithms for model based decision making, machine learning, deep learning, artificial intelligence
  • brain-controlled vehicles, intelligent autonomous systems, intelligent ground-air-water vehicles, driverless and driver assisted vehicles, cooperating robotic systems, robotic systems and swarms, automotive systems
  • aerospace systems, spacecraft, hypersonic vehicles, high-performance aircraft, drones, quadrotors, helicopters, tilt-wing and tilt-rotor rotorcraft, micro- and nano-air vehicles

  • internet of things, cloud computing, optimization (nonlinear, convex, etc.), 3D animation and games, additive manufacturing
